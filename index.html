The Purification Process

The Black Berkey filters are designed to remove or dramatically reduce a broad range of contaminants. These contaminants go through a process called ionic "absorption". This method processes the water through mictropores through the filters shell in convoluted twists known as the "tortuous path", further ensnaring the tiniest of single-called organisms and pollutants.

Black Berkey Filters remove patogenic bacteria (and surrogates) to >99.9999% and viruses to >99.9999% (Log 5)
